About Us

Established in 2000, R.M. Poly & Packaging is one of Bangladesh’s leading manufacturers in the plastic and packaging industry. We proudly serve the ice cream, food, pharmaceutical, and garments sectors with high-quality, reliable, and innovative packaging solutions.

Our Mission

To deliver innovative, safe, and sustainable packaging solutions that empower our clients’ growth. We achieve this through advanced technology, industry expertise, and exceptional customer service that ensures long-term partnerships.

Our Vision

To lead Bangladesh’s packaging industry by setting new benchmarks in automation, IML innovation, and eco-friendly production. Our goal is to become the most trusted partner for quality-driven brands across food, pharmaceuticals, and beyond.

IML Technology

Side Entry IML directly fuses labels into the mold, producing hygienic, durable, and premium-quality plastic packaging. This innovative process improves food, ice cream, and pharmaceutical packaging while boosting brand visibility.

Thin Wall

Thin Wall Machine creates lightweight yet strong containers, reducing material use while ensuring safety and durability. Ideal for food storage, pharma, & goods, this eco-friendly solution delivers cost efficiency and sustainability.

High-Speed Plastic Packaging Machines

Our high-speed injection molding machines ensure rapid, precise production, making them ideal for pharmaceutical and food packaging where consistency, safety, and hygiene are critical.

IMM Robotic Automation

IMM Robots automate product removal, improving speed, operator safety, and handling accuracy while minimizing contamination risks or damage.

100% Food-Grade Resin

We use only 100% virgin food-grade resin that meets pharmaceutical and food safety standards, ensuring contamination-free and reliable packaging.

Precision Machine Control

Advanced machine control systems regulate temperature, pressure, and cycle time, guaranteeing consistent quality packaging in every batch.
